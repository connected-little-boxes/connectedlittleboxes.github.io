Lights in Names

A light with your name in

If you've ever wanted to see your name in lights, now you can. We've taken the Connected Little Boxes technology and put it in a box with your name on, with a light for each letter. Your name could go red when the weather is warm, or green when your'e approachable, or twinkle when you're happy. It's up to you.

Lights in Names Designer

We've got a nifty bit of software that generates designs for 3d printing so that you can make a name for yourself.

You can find the software, and instructions telling you how to use it, here.
